In the remote Indian Ocean lies the Saya de Malha Bank, a vast underwater plateau the size of Belgium. It is home to the world's largest seagrass meadow, as well as shallow corals and an abundance of marine life. Endangered turtles, sperm whales, and even pygmy blue whales can be found in these waters.
