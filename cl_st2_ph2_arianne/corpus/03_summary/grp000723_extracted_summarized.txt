In 2020, amid adapting to COVID-19 security measures and remote work privileges, the author highlights the essential role of public transport workers in enabling frontline workers without cars to reach their destinations. Often overlooked due to societal emphasis on individual transport like cars and flying as symbols of freedom, mobility is vital for community care, sustenance, and addressing inequality. COVID underscores the need to invest in strong public services and express gratitude to their providers. The post calls for appreciating and thanking public transport staff—drivers, ticket sellers, technicians, operators, cleaners, maintenance, and office workers—for keeping communities running, especially during the holidays. #ThanksForKeepingUsMoving
