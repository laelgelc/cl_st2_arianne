The blog post traces the history of environmental awareness from ancient times, citing early examples like Vedic hymns, Taoist principles, and Mesopotamian epics warning of ecological harm. It highlights medieval pollution concerns, 18th-19th century figures like Franklin, Thoreau, and Haeckel who advanced ecology and conservation, and 20th-century milestones including Carson's Silent Spring, anti-nuclear campaigns, the Chipko movement, and the founding of Greenpeace in 1969. It culminates with the Rainbow Warrior and notes ongoing global environmental challenges.
