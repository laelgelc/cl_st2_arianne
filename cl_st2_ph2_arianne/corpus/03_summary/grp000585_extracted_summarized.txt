The author recounts joining Greenpeace Africa in 2011 after journalism in Cameroon, progressing from Communications Manager to Head of Communications. Key highlights include opposing nuclear expansion in South Africa, leading to its abandonment in 2019; exposing illegal fishing in West Africa via the Rainbow Warrior, resulting in license revocations; launching a report in Senegal on overfishing's impacts, influencing policy and migration narratives; and protesting coal dependence, promoting renewables and a Just Transition. Amid COVID-19, they reflect on combating corruption, deforestation in the Congo Basin, and persisting in narrative change for environmental justice.
