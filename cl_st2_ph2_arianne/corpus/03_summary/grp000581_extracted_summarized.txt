The blog post recounts the author's experiences as a Greenpeace photographer from 1974 to 1982, sharing photographs and memories of campaigns. It describes chartering ships to confront whaling fleets, disrupting Russian whalers in 1975-1976, halting harp seal hunts off Labrador while compromising with local and Indigenous hunters, anti-nuclear actions including blocking Rocky Flats and Seabrook with allies like Allen Ginsberg, a "test blockade" of an oil tanker in 1981, visits to abandoned whaling stations, and the global media impact of their whale confrontation footage.
