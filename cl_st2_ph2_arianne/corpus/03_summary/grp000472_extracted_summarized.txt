The blog post discusses climate anxiety, noting a 2021 study where 84% of young people are worried and over 45% report daily impacts. It recommends podcasts for coping: "Climate and You" by Domyo Burk explores emotions and staying engaged without despair; "Climate Change and Happiness" by Panu Pihkala and Thomas Doherty encourages articulating fears and finding ways through; "How to Save A Planet" offers energizing, humorous stories on action, like Ayana Elizabeth Johnson's Venn diagram and episodes with Britt Wray; "Outrage + Optimism" balances justified outrage with optimism, featuring guests like David Attenborough and Luisa Neubauer on youth anxiety studies.
