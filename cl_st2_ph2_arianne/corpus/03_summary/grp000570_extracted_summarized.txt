The blog post recounts extreme weather events in 2021, including record cold waves in Madrid and Texas, Cyclone Eloise devastating Mozambique and nearby countries, Beijing's severe sandstorm, floods in Australia and Europe, wildfires in Turkey, Greece, and California, flash floods in Asia, Amazon deforestation, and fires in Patagonia. It highlights how climate change exacerbates these, with personal anecdotes of ignoring warnings until impacts hit close. It urges phasing out fossil fuels, transitioning to renewables, and holding governments and companies accountable to address the climate emergency.
