Aliyah Field, aboard the Polar Pioneer in the Pacific Ocean, prepares to protest Shell's Arctic oil drilling by scaling an oil rig, overcoming personal fears and discomforts. She highlights the environmental risks, including threats to clean air, stable weather, beaches, and a habitable future for new generations. Urging readers to join her, five teammates, and over 6 million supporters, she stresses that collective determination will succeed in halting corporate greed, regardless of individual obstacles.
