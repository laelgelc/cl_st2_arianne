The blog post highlights the climate crisis driven by fossil fuels, criticizing companies and politicians for empty promises, lies, and false solutions that sabotage emission cuts, harming the vulnerable. It calls for banning fossil fuel propaganda through a European Citizens Initiative requiring 1 million signatures to prohibit ads and sponsorships for fossil fuels, related transport, and involved companies, aiming for a #FossilFreeRevolution. Comparing Big Oil to Big Tobacco, it accuses firms like Shell and BP of denying responsibility, greenwashing, and delaying action via marketing. Consequences include deadly air pollution, species extinction, and extreme weather displacing millions.
