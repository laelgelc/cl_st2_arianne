In 2016, the author visited Ecuador's Amazon, witnessing Indigenous and farmer communities poisoned by Chevron's oil waste, which caused cancer, birth defects, and contamination exceeding legal standards. After winning a $9.5 billion judgment in Ecuadorian courts, upheld on appeals, Chevron refused payment and launched retaliatory attacks on lawyer Steven Donziger, including a US RICO case under Judge Kaplan. Kaplan accepted false testimony from a paid witness, barred pollution evidence, and imposed home detention on Donziger for contempt, highlighting corporate intimidation against environmental advocates.
