The blog post highlights similarities between the unique Amazon Reef off Brazil and the Great Southern Reef off Australia's southern coast, both supporting diverse species like pink dolphins, leafy sea dragons, whales, and sea lions. It discusses threats from oil companies, including Total's denied drilling license near the Amazon Reef and Equinor's delayed plans in the Great Australian Bight due to community opposition and environmental risks like spills. Greenpeace campaigns involve global defenders, scientists, and locals to protect these ecosystems from fossil fuels, advocating for clean energy and urging readers to join the movement.
