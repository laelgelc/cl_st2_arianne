Greenpeace's Arctic Sunrise conducted a four-month voyage in the Indian Ocean to investigate destructive fishing and research ecosystems, highlighting stories from Mauritius, Seychelles, and Madagascar. The post discusses threats like industrial fishing, pollution, and climate breakdown harming wildlife, ecosystems, and communities' livelihoods. It introduces the Vital Ocean Voices project, which grants support to local individuals and NGOs to share firsthand accounts through writing, videos, animations, and poems. Examples include Kylian's oil spill response, Madame Kokoly's fishing experiences, Aurelie's ocean connection, Seychelles mangrove rehabilitation, and school pupils' environmental actions.
