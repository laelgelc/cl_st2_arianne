The blog post highlights international outrage over Putin's invasion of Ukraine and calls for solidarity through donations, protests, and support for refugees. It urges banks and investors like Credit Suisse, Schroders, Pictet, East Capital, Carnegie Fonder, Intesa SanPaolo, UniCredit, Société Générale, Raiffeisen Bank International, and UBS to stop funding Russian fossil fuels, which finance the war. Protests target banks in Vienna and Czech Republic. It emphasizes redirecting money from conflict-fueling fossil fuels to renewables for peace, noting European institutions' role in building Putin's power.
