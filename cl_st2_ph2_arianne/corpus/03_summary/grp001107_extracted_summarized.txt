Greenpeace celebrates over one million people worldwide urging corporations to end single-use plastics, highlighting local bans and community cleanups that audit collected waste to hold companies accountable. The post introduces the "Million Acts of Blue" goal, defining an Act of Blue as any action preventing single-use plastic creation, inspired by planetary protection and corporate accountability. It promotes a toolkit with guides for community actions like presentations, media outreach, targeting supermarkets for reduced plastic use, and urging fast food places to eliminate throwaway items.
