The 2020 Greenpeace film festival features 15 free documentaries on environmental issues like climate, food, agriculture, overconsumption, biodiversity, whistleblowers, and fossil energies, available in English and French. Viewers act as the jury, voting for the People's Choice Award. Films explore topics such as Borneo's forest destruction, rewilding European landscapes, sea freight's impact, saving the Amazon, rural French challenges, ocean preservation, planetary crises, Indigenous resistance to oil in New Zealand, plastic pollution on Basque beaches, eggs and African migration, endangered French forests, Kiribati climate refugees, U.S. climate fights, daily biodiversity actions, and reforming industrial agriculture.
