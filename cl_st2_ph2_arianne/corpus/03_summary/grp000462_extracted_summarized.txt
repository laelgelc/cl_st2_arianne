A photo editor describes the mental health toll of viewing images of global climate disasters exacerbated by climate change. Recent events include devastating floods in Pakistan submerging a third of the country and displacing millions despite low emissions; unprecedented rains in Bangalore, India, causing widespread damage due to poor planning; flooding displacing over 125,000 in West and Central Africa, with 300 deaths in Nigeria; Europe's worst drought drying rivers; Chile's ongoing drought worsened by water privatization affecting half its population; and typhoons/hurricanes in Asia, Puerto Rico, and Canada. Amid frustration with fossil fuel profits, youth marches offer hope for ending dependence on them.
