The blog post highlights severe climate crisis impacts in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA), including lives lost, destroyed homes, failing crops, and threatened livelihoods, with the region warming twice the global average. It details unprecedented droughts, floods, heatwaves, and other extremes in countries like Algeria, Egypt, Morocco, Tunisia, and the UAE. The post urges transitioning from fossil fuels to renewables for energy independence and criticizes historic polluters for refusing loss and damage payments. It calls for COP27 to establish a grant-based finance facility for adaptation, mitigation, and compensation to enable recovery and a greener future.
