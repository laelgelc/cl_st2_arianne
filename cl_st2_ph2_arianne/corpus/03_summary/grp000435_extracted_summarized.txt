Greenpeace highlights the vital role of volunteers worldwide, who engage in activities like organizing demonstrations, public speaking, and non-violent direct actions to protect the planet. Profiles feature individuals such as Christina from Chile, concerned about droughts and water access; Korean actor Junghoe Kim, addressing plastic waste; Katerina from Greece, fighting oil drilling; Marcos from Argentina, tackling climate change and forest fires; Kely from Brazil, focused on Amazon deforestation; and Lu Chia-Ling from Taiwan, advocating for marine conservation. They share experiences, motivations, and hopes for global environmental changes, inspiring others to volunteer.
