The Greenpeace blog post discusses the challenges of self-isolation during the COVID-19 pandemic, emphasizing the need to maintain physical and mental health while upholding guiding values. It expresses gratitude to essential workers like nurses, doctors, and delivery drivers for their bravery. The post recommends environmental films and series, including Leonardo DiCaprio's journey witnessing climate change, the founding of Greenpeace, Sylvia Earle's oceanographic achievements, a documentary on minimalism, stories of Brazilians impacted by climate crises, a film debunking meat consumption myths featuring Arnold Schwarzenegger, "Sexy Killer" exposing Indonesia's coal industry scandals, and a documentary on coral bleaching in Australia. It invites readers to share their favorite environmental films.
