The blog post celebrates Valencia's commitment to double organic farming land from 8% to 20% by 2020, backed by 23 million Euros, amid Spain's economic crisis and high pesticide use harming bees. It highlights growing global demand for ecological food, farmers shifting from industrial systems, and a burgeoning movement including markets and co-ops. The author urges government investment in ecological agriculture to combat climate change, pollution, biodiversity loss, and hunger, while calling on individuals to pledge support for better food.
