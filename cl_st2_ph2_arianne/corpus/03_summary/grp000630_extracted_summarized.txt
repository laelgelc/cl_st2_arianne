The blog post discusses the intensifying climate crisis as outlined in the IPCC report, evidenced by recent wildfires, floods, and heatwaves, urging urgent action. It explores photography's role in raising awareness without shocking audiences, emphasizing engaging, honest images that start conversations and evoke emotion. Photographers share personal narratives, such as Lake Urmia's shrinkage, Sri Lanka's environmental threats, and Arctic scientific work, highlighting shifts in media attention and the need for balanced depictions of urgency and hope. The EverydayClimateChange project expands via exhibitions and YouTube, while Greenpeace stresses bearing witness to drive change.
