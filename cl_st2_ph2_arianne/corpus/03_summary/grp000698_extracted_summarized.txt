Greenpeace's blog post highlights how grassroots movements drive social change and introduces a series of live online Roundtable discussions co-created with activists, scientists, and others to explore themes like diversity, solidarity, and intersectionality. It details panels on: health impacts of the climate crisis in vulnerable regions, featuring experts from MSF and others; activism in the Amazon, discussing threats and protection needs; activist burnout and resilience strategies; and intergenerational activism, emphasizing cross-generational alliances for environmental and social justice. It invites collaboration and quotes panelists on interconnected issues.
