In November, positive environmental developments included Greece achieving its first full reliance on renewable energy for five hours on October 5, marking progress in reducing fossil fuel dependence. New York advanced a ban on new gasoline-powered vehicles by 2035, mandating increasing zero-emission sales starting at 35% in 2026. Equinor postponed the Arctic's Wisting oil field, likely preventing its development and averting CO2 emissions. Jakarta residents won a second air pollution lawsuit appeal, obligating the government to set air quality standards and strategies. French President Macron endorsed banning deep sea mining at COP27, strengthening opposition to the practice.
