On World Environment Day, the post highlights that over 9 out of 10 people worldwide breathe unsafe air, framing air pollution as a global health and environmental crisis impacting all breathing species. It questions whether the day is for celebration and urges demanding the right to clean air, clean energy, and a greener environment on June 5. Though often invisible, pollution's health effects are evident and undeniable; if visible, it would mar the world's beauty. The post invites exploring coal energy's journey from underground extraction to powering homes while choking lives. Text by Manjari Sharma, images by Fiona Porteous.
