Indigenous Land Defenders in the Amazon face increasing threats to their lives and homes while protecting vital ecosystems. Greenpeace supports Indigenous rights globally and demands an end to profit-driven destruction. On May 26, illegal gold miners, emboldened by Brazil's Bolsonaro administration, attacked a Munduruku village with gunfire and arson, burning leader Maria Leusa's home. This follows ongoing violence, including the March destruction of an Indigenous women's association headquarters and attacks on Yanomami people.
