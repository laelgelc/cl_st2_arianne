The blog post critiques the economic system of "Fossil Capital," which prioritizes endless growth over people and the planet, plundering resources, oppressing the vulnerable, and deepening inequalities and the climate crisis fueled by fossil fuels. It highlights how the 2008 financial crisis led to austerity measures that sacrificed the future, exacerbating crises now worsened by COVID-19, with the most oppressed bearing the brunt while the powerful benefit. Impacts include climate deaths, hardships for youth, women, low-income communities, and biodiversity loss. Quoting activist Kaossara Sani, it calls for action to break the system and launch a #FossilFreeRevolution.
