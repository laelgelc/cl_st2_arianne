In 2022, notable environmental wins included the Philippines' Commission on Human Rights declaring climate change a human rights issue and holding corporations accountable; the EU proposing a law against SLAPPs to protect advocates; the UN General Assembly affirming the right to a healthy environment; California banning new oil and gas wells near communities; the EU's deforestation law requiring supply chain traceability; Canada rejecting an Arctic iron mine expansion to protect Inuit lands; global calls for a deep sea mining moratorium; a UN resolution for a plastics treaty; and court victories in Argentina, South Africa, and Indonesia against oil exploration and for clean air.
