Lagi Toribau reflects on 2020 as a year of disruption, highlighting a broken global system favoring competition, power, and inequality over collaboration and human life. Drawing from his Fijian upbringing in communal structures, he advocates for mindset change to enable societal transformation. He envisions a world without biases, categorizations, or competition, where happiness and health are universal currencies, and humans live in harmony with nature. COVID-19 exposes system vulnerabilities, urging expanded security, equity, and environmental protection. He addresses systemic racism, inspired by George Floyd's murder and Black Lives Matter, emphasizing individual choices for justice.
