Dr. Paul Johnston, a Greenpeace scientist, discusses the severe impacts of climate change, noting the planet will survive but in a altered state, with committed changes like sea level rise and food insecurity. He emphasizes acting now to minimize damage, rejects climate denial as delusional, warns against geoengineering as a false solution, and highlights progress in environmental protections. He advocates personal actions to reduce waste and influence others, supports the March for Science to defend evidence-based research, and expresses optimism amid challenges like fake news and funding cuts.
