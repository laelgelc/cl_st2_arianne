The blog post highlights the intensifying climate emergencies of 2020, urging collective action to combat climate change. It details Hurricane Laura's devastation in the US, causing deaths and widespread power outages; Siberia's heat waves, oil spills, and forest fires; Amazon fires threatening the rainforest and global temperatures; California's prolonged fire seasons destroying lives and ecosystems; and back-to-back typhoons in the Philippines leading to floods, displacement, and calls for a climate emergency. Amid a pandemic with temporary emission reductions, 2020 remains one of the hottest years, marked by Australian bushfires, and floods in Kenya, Japan, China, Indonesia, and South Korea causing deaths and massive damage.
