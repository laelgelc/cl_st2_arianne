Seoul-based celebrity photographer Cho Sun-Hee has collaborated with Greenpeace Korea on a photoshoot for W magazine, aiming to leverage celebrities' influence to raise awareness about environmental issues. The project sought to highlight the problems of single-use plastics and the impacts of climate change, including heatwaves, typhoons, and altered seasons.

Cho Sun-Hee emphasizes the importance of limiting disposables and believes in the necessity of government and corporate action. She also points to the need for public education, especially within schools, to inspire individual efforts. To visualize these messages, the photoshoot used digital editing to depict challenges like rising sea levels as well as solutions like renewable energy.

The project deepened her personal commitment, which is influenced by her son. She also highlighted the sincere participation of the celebrities involved.
