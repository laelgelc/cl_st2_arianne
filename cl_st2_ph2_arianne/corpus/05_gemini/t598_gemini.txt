On World Environment Day, it is important to note that over 9 out of 10 people worldwide breathe unsafe air. Air pollution is a global health and environmental crisis that impacts all breathing species. This reality leads to a question: is this day one for celebration?

On June 5, we should be demanding the right to clean air, clean energy, and a greener environment. Although pollution is often invisible, its health effects are evident and undeniable. If it were visible, it would mar the world's beauty.

You are invited to explore the journey of coal energy, from its extraction underground to the way it powers homes while choking lives.
