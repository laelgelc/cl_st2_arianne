This decision to expand Arctic drilling directly contradicts the global warming limits set by the recent Paris Agreement. It also runs counter to a sustainability deal on the Arctic made between Norway and the US. In response, civil society groups, including Greenpeace, are seeking a constitutional review of the new licenses, citing environmental rights. This challenge could potentially lead to court action, offering hope for a future that prioritizes a shift to renewables over the expansion of Arctic oil.
