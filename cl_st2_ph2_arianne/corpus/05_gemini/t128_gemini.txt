Our campaign to protect the Argentine Sea from the threat of offshore oil development continues. These projects overlap with the habitats of protected species, including the Southern Right Whale.

Despite a precautionary measure that had suspended these activities, a recent government resolution has approved the Environmental Impact Assessment submitted by the oil company Equinor. This decision violates the Escaz√∫ Agreement, which guarantees environmental rights and public participation. In response, we have submitted a formal letter demanding the annulment of this resolution.

We continue to stand with the ongoing protests, including the #Atlanticazo demonstrations, to voice our opposition. The company pushing for this development, Equinor, has a history of oil spills and leaks in its operations in Norway and Brazil. The dangers are clear: seismic explorations and potential spills threaten to cause immense ecological and economic disasters in our waters.
