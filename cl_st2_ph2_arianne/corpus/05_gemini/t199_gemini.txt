Russia's invasion of Ukraine is condemned, posing risks to people and causing immense suffering in cities like Kyiv, Kharkiv, Kherson, and Mariupol. Over 1.5 million people have fled, leading to a major humanitarian crisis.

There are organizations that can be supported:
*   UNHCR provides support for refugees.
*   Africans Rising assists stranded Africans.
*   Stand with Ukraine organizes protests.
*   War Help aids people who are fleeing.
*   The Ukrainian Red Cross delivers aid.
*   People in Need provides supplies.
*   OutRight Action supports LGBTQIA+ groups.
*   The Kyiv Independent offers reporting.
*   Blood Agents facilitates blood donations.
*   Unicef helps children and families.

This was authored by Safina Okumu and Jamie Woolley from Greenpeace.
