Six months ago, I travelled with a group of journalists to visit the Geraizeiros community in Bahia, Brazil. Our time there was violently interrupted by the arrival of armed men. Posing as police officers, they brandished assault rifles and intimidated us, demanding entry without producing any warrants or identification. After two hours, they left, but not before threatening to return. Their actions have left the community in a state of fear.
