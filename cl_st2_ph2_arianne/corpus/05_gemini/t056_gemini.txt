In a collaborative effort, Greenpeace CEE has partnered with Ukrainian organizations Ecoaction, Ecoclub, and Victory of Ukraine to reconstruct a war-damaged hospital in Horenka, located near Kyiv.

The project centered on installing a heat pump with 65-meter ground probes and a hybrid solar power system. This sustainable upgrade enhances the hospital's energy independence while reducing CO2 emissions. The new system is projected to cut heating costs by 80% and can cover up to 60% of the facility's energy needs.
