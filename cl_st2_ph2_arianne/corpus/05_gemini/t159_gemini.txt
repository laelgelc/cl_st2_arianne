Activists have disrupted the African Energy Summit in London, highlighting the issue of neo-colonial oil extraction in Africa which serves to exacerbate poverty in formerly colonized nations.

Attention is centered on the Canadian firm ReconAfrica and its planned drilling operations in the Kavango region of Namibia and Botswana. This area includes the Okavango Delta. The project risks generating massive CO2 emissions, an amount equivalent to one-sixth of the global carbon budget, which would breach the goals set by the Paris Agreement.
