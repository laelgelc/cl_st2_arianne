Around the world, Greenpeace offices have documented extreme weather events. In Japan, Germany, Denmark, Switzerland, and Korea, extreme heatwaves have been recorded, with some breaking records. These conditions have led to devastating forest fires across Russia, Greece, and California, affecting millions of people and causing fatalities.

At the same time, flooding has increased in southern France and India. In the Philippines, a recent storm worsened the plastic pollution crisis, as waste clogged drains and washed ashore.
