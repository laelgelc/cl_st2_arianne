Women like Montse, Pilar, and Remedios highlight the role of the Coordinadora in uniting local groups. Their work aims to inform cities, coordinate efforts against large companies, and amplify rural voices. Remedios fought against a pig farm in her town. Greenpeace Spain is joining this cause to highlight the organizing efforts of these women against industrial farming.
