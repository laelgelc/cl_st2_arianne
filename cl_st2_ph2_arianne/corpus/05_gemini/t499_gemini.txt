In Australia, hundreds of people have been left stranded on beaches, surrounded by raging fires. Millions of hectares of forest have been reduced to ashes overnight. Over a few short months, the toll on wildlife has been immense, with an estimated loss of about a billion animals.

This is a serious climate emergency. The time for mere discussion has passed; these events demand immediate action.

We invite you to look at the accompanying images. They are a witness to the large-scale catastrophe that has been unfolding in Australia in recent months.
