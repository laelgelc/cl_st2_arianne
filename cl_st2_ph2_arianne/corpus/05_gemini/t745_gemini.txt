We must engage in civil disobedience against the financial systems fueling climate change. Greenpeace Sweden is refusing to pay portions of employer pension contributions to a state fund that is invested in fossil fuels. The money is instead being deposited in a safe account, where it will be held until policies are aligned with the Paris Agreement.

There is a warning that stranded fossil fuel assets risk causing an economic meltdown similar to the one in 2008. Global divestment efforts by institutions and individuals must be scaled up. A United Nations report has cited the need for $90 trillion for renewable solutions to meet climate goals. We encourage readers to sign the petition.
