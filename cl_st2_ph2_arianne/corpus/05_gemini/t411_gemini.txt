Greenpeace Denmark activist Ida Marie has recounted her experience peacefully occupying the Dan Bravo oil rig in the North Sea. As part of a campaign against oil and gas exploration, she and three others swam 500 meters from the Rainbow Warrior ship to the rig, where they remained for 36 hours.

To prepare for the action, Ida Marie trained for a month, undertaking physical challenges that included swimming with heavy bags and climbing ladders.
