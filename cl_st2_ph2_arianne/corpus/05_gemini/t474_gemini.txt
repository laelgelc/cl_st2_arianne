The impacts of COVID-19 on daily life call for a focus on systemic solutions, alongside the promotion of community care rooted in love and compassion. There are eleven key ways to safely support others. We can use alternatives to social distancing to stay connected, check on individuals who are isolated, and offer practical assistance like shopping deliveries. It is vital to combat racism against those of Chinese descent and to appreciate government financial aid where it is provided.

We can also help by countering misinformation, suggesting that landlords implement rent freezes, and fundraising for those affected by the crisis. Hosting virtual celebrations and sharing positive stories can help maintain morale. When preparing, we should do so without hoarding, and remember the importance of frequent handwashing.

This crisis has exposed existing inequalities. As we move forward, we must call against bailouts for the fossil fuel industry. Instead, we should advocate for a green stimulus aimed at building a resilient and caring society.
