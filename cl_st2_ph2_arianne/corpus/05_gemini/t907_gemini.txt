Foreign vessels are depleting local tuna stocks, which harms local economies. In 2014, a record 4.8 million tonnes of tuna were caught, and three-quarters of bigeye and yellowfin stocks are now overfished.

Furthermore, unregulated FADs pollute the marine environment with plastics and batteries, often washing up on beaches and damaging coral reefs. Citizens have found discarded fishing gear and successfully linked it to Thai Union.
