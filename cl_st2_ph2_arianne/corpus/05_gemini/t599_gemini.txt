Millions of school students across the world have been protesting, not for higher fees or for jobs, but out of a love for the planet and a deep concern over climate change. Reflecting on my own school days, there was a lack of such awareness. This is an unprecedented and organic movement on a global scale, driven by the students' clear vision, honest intentions, and environmental worries.

Tech-savvy millennials have revived street protests with a new creativity. They have crafted delightful banners and redefined the art of slogan-writing, becoming a part of history in the process. Among the top banner messages, one standout was, "May the force be with you."
