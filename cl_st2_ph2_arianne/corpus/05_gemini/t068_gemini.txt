Twitter serves as a platform for expressing climate concerns, but it presents challenges such as information overload and the rise of climate disinformation, with an increase in "climate scam" tweets. Amidst this, favorite climate tweets from 2022 remind us that we are not alone in feeling overwhelmed by extreme weather, unheard in our advocacy, or anxious about the future.

It is important not to give up the fight. We must urge global leaders to act, acknowledging that vulnerable nations like Pakistan, Bangladesh, and African countries are facing the crisis despite having low emissions. The planet must be valued over the profits of fossil fuels. We thank the climate experts who work to counter denialism. Hope is essential to inspire better actions.
