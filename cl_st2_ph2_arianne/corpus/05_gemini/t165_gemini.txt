In Amsterdam, Greenpeace volunteers from 16 countries stood with Brazilian Indigenous leaders to protest by blocking a ship. The vessel was carrying 60 million kilos of soy from Brazil. The demonstrators are demanding a robust EU anti-deforestation law to protect forests, ecosystems, and the rights of Indigenous peoples.

The current draft of the law has gaps. It excludes areas like the Cerrado and offers weak protections for human rights. This is happening while corporations resist transparency.
