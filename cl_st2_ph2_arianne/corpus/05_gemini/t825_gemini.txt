In response to a lawsuit by Resolute Forest Products against Greenpeace and Stand.Earth for advocating for the protection of the boreal forest, a movement of global solidarity has emerged. People in over 25 countries have gathered around iconic trees to oppose Resolute's efforts to silence critics and stifle free speech.
