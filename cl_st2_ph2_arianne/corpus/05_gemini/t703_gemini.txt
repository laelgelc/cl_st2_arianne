Emma Thompson has recounted a frightening encounter she had with an orangutan in a sanctuary in Kalimantan. This experience highlights how deforestation for palm oil plantations is destroying habitats. In just 16 years, 100,000 orangutans have been killed by being shot, starved, bulldozed, or burned in fires.

This destruction also endangers other species, including Sumatran tigers, rhinos, and elephants. Furthermore, it leads to human suffering through land conflicts and rights abuses.
