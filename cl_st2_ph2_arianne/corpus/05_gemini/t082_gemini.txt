Volunteers across the world are vital to the work of protecting our planet. They engage in a variety of activities, including organizing demonstrations, public speaking, and taking part in non-violent direct actions.

Profiles of these individuals highlight their dedication. Christina from Chile is motivated by her concerns about droughts and access to water. In Korea, actor Junghoe Kim is working to address the problem of plastic waste. Katerina from Greece is fighting against oil drilling, while Marcos in Argentina tackles climate change and forest fires. In Brazil, Kely is focused on Amazon deforestation, and Lu Chia-Ling from Taiwan advocates for marine conservation.

These volunteers share their experiences, motivations, and hopes for achieving global environmental changes. By doing so, they inspire others to join the movement and volunteer.
