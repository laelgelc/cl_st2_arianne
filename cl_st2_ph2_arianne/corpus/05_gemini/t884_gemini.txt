Progress has been made in the fashion industry's journey toward toxic-free production by 2020. While some brands resisted, initial commitments were secured from Adidas, Puma, and Nike. Today, over 70 brands, which account for 15% of global textile production, have committed to Detox, proving that the goal is feasible.

To measure this progress, the Detox Catwalk ranks 19 companies. H&M, Zara (Inditex), and Benetton are leading the pack as "Detox Avant-Garde" for their work on chemical bans and transparency.
