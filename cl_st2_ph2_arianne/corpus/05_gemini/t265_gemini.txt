During a visit to Brindisi's beaches on the Adriatic coast, extensive plastic waste was observed. A bleach bottle from the 1960s, made by Montecatini, was found, highlighting how plastic persists in nature for decades.

The local petrochemical plant, now under the ownership of ENI's Versalis, manufactures plastic pellets using oil and gas. This facility is a source of pollution, contributing to gas flaring and benzene emissions.
