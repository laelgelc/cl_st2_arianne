Despite its image as a nature-loving country, Norway is failing to protect the oceans. The nation has positioned itself as an opponent to a strong Global Ocean Treaty. This opposition is part of a pattern; in 2016, Norway blocked the creation of protected areas in the Central Arctic Ocean, and last year, it worked with Russia and China to block protections in the Antarctic.
