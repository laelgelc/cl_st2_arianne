Following years of Greenpeace campaigns at CCAMLR, the world's largest marine protected area has been created in Antarctica's Ross Sea. This achievement comes amid other recent ocean protections, such as the expansions in Papahanaumokuakea and the creation of new monuments. Now, the focus must turn towards establishing high seas sanctuaries through action at the United Nations.
