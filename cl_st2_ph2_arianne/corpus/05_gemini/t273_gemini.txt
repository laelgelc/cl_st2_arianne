Fossil fuel companies are using soft power through sponsorships and influence to greenwash their reputations and maintain their climate-damaging business models.

This strategy can be seen across sports, where companies like Ineos and Total sponsor rugby events, and Gazprom holds a partnership with UEFA. The industry's reach extends into cultural spaces as well. Shell has funded museum exhibitions that were bound by non-criticism clauses, while ExxonMobil has supported the arts even as it actively denied climate science.
