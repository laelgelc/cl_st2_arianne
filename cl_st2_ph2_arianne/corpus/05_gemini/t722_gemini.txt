Following global campaigns by Greenpeace, Samsung Electronics has committed to powering its operations in the US, China, and Europe with 100% renewable energy by 2020. This commitment includes its manufacturing factories.

To support this goal, the company plans onsite solar and geothermal installations near its Korean semiconductor plants in Hwaseong, Pyongtaek, and Suwon. In doing so, Samsung is also supporting South Korea's national target of achieving 20% renewable energy by 2030. Furthermore, Samsung will join the CDP supply chain program next year as a measure to address climate risks.
