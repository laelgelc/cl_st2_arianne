Nine inspiring climate activists from around the world are using social media to raise awareness about climate issues and motivate action.

From Uganda, Edwin Namakanga was inspired by climate impacts and activists like Vanessa Nakate. Fabian Grischkat from Germany advocates for both climate and LGBTQ+ rights. Lillian Ono from Japan links climate to systemic injustices. Also from Uganda, Rose Kobusinge focuses on amplifying African voices and promoting sustainable energy.

Tori Tsui, from Hong Kong and the UK, addresses the intersections of mental health and climate. Venetia La Manna, based in the UK, challenges the fast fashion industry. From Brazil, Amanda Costa connects the issues of climate and racial justice. Ayisha Siddiqa from Pakistan fights polluters and advocates for marginalized communities. In France, Camille Etienne lobbies against pipelines and mining.
