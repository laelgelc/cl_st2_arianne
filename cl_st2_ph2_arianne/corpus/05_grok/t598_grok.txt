On World Environment Day, it's crucial to recognize that over 9 out of 10 people worldwide are breathing unsafe air. This positions air pollution as a profound global health and environmental crisis that affects every breathing species on the planet.

But is this a day for celebration? Instead, let's use June 5 to demand our right to clean air, clean energy, and a greener environment.

Air pollution is often invisible, yet its health impacts are clear and impossible to ignore. If we could see it, it would obscure the beauty of our world.
