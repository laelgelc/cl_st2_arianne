Record-breaking cold, ice, and snow have swept across the Northern Hemisphere, impacting East Asia, Europe, the US, Texas, and northern Mexico. Power grid failures in Texas and Mexico have been blamed on fossil fuels rather than renewables.

The polar vortex is a swirling ring of cold air in the stratosphere that weakens and wobbles due to Arctic warming from the climate crisis, leading to more frequent extreme winters.

We must phase out all fossil fuels and shift to 100% renewable energy. Governments and corporations need to take urgent action. Commitments have come from China, Japan, and Korea. Join Greenpeace's campaign to participate.
