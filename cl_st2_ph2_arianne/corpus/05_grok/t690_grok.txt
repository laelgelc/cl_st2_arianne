Environmental defenders around the world are facing criminalization, threats, surveillance, and violence from governments and corporations. Their movements are being labeled as terrorism and targeted with lawsuits. According to Global Witness, killings of these defenders increased to 207 in 2017, up from 201 in 2016 and 154 in 2015, with 66 more reported in 2018.
