In 2017, Zhao joined Greenpeace East Asia as an intern and later became a researcher. In this role, she supports climate campaigns by utilizing GIS and satellite analysis.

She points out the widespread public misinformation surrounding climate change and stresses the urgent need for greater awareness. Zhao has personally witnessed the retreat of glaciers on the Tibetan Plateau since 2008.

In urban environments, it's easy to lose sight of humanity's fundamental reliance on nature. This disconnection persists amid ongoing deforestation, habitat loss, and the degradation of the Amazon, which has shifted from a carbon sink to a carbon emitter.

Even in moments of despair, Zhao finds hope by drawing on Jane Goodall's message to take action.
