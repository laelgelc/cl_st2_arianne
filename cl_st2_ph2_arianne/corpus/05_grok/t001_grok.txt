Roger Payne, who passed away in 2023 at the age of 88, made a profound impact on ocean conservation through his groundbreaking work. In 1970, he recorded "Songs of the Humpback Whale," an album that sold over 100,000 copies and sparked the global anti-whaling movement, including inspiring Greenpeace's efforts.

Payne's contributions extended to founding Ocean Alliance and playing a key role in securing the 1982 moratorium on commercial whaling.
