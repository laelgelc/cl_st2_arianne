Young climate activists are stepping up to challenge world leaders on their inaction against the climate crisis. In Australia, students have staged school walkouts to protest government policies, including support for coal.

Jamie Margolin founded Zero Hour and organized the Youth Climate March. She also sued Washington state over climate rights violations.

Greta Thunberg started school strikes in Sweden, which inspired global movements. She delivered a powerful speech at COP24.

Litia, a Pacific Islander and survivor of Cyclone Winston, advocates for ending fossil fuels through Pacific Island Represent.

Marinel Ubaldo, a survivor of Typhoon Haiyan from the Philippines, testifies against major polluters, emphasizing corporate responsibility.
