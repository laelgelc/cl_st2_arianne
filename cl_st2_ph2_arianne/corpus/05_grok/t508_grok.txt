At COP25, a groundbreaking moment is unfolding: the first intercontinental meeting of Climate Strikers from the global north and south. Representatives from each continent are coming together to amplify youth voices in climate policy decisions.

In the past, conferences like this have excluded young people, leaving their perspectives sidelined. But this time, things will be different. The strikers are taking the initiative with actions such as a press conference on Wednesday and a massive climate march in Madrid on Friday, which will feature Greta Thunberg.
