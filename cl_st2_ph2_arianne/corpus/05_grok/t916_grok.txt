Today marks the International Day of Forests on March 21, a perfect moment to celebrate the vital role forests play in our world and to take action for their protection. As we honor these ecosystems that sustain life, let's remember that one powerful step we can all take is reducing our meat consumption to help curb the pressures driving deforestation.
