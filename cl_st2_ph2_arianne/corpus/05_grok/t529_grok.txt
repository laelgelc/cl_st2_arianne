Greenpeace is campaigning worldwide against the oil industry to combat the climate emergency. In Australia, our efforts are defending the Great Australian Bight from Equinor's drilling. In Brazil and French Guiana, we're protecting the Amazon Reef from BP. In Germany, we're protesting internal combustion engines. In Russia, we're supporting Indigenous opposition to Surgutneftegas. In Canada, we're fighting tar sands pipelines. In New Zealand, we're opposing OMV's permits. In Norway, we're challenging Arctic drilling legally. In the UK, we're blocking BP rigs. In the Philippines, we're blockading Shell and investigating fossil fuel accountability. In the US, we're pushing for a Green New Deal and halting expansions. Millions are uniting for a transition to clean energy.
