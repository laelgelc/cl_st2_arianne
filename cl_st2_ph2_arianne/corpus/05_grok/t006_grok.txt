Climate crises don't affect everyone equally, and LGBTQIA2S+ individuals often face disproportionate discrimination that worsens inequalities in housing, healthcare, and access to resources. This reality underscores the deep interconnectedness between queer and climate movements, calling for unity to protect both people and the planet. At Greenpeace, we stress the importance of fighting for all, and it's worth noting that the rainbow flag includes green to represent nature.

Key challenges include significantly higher homelessness rates among LGBTQIA2S+ people.
