As a volunteer deckhand on the Greenpeace ship Arctic Sunrise, I've been sailing in the Sylt Outer Reef marine sanctuary off Germany's coast. On board, I've learned nautical terms like bulkheads for walls and deckheads for ceilings. The constant wobbling at sea affects daily life in areas such as the mess, which is the dining room, and the galley, which is the kitchen.
