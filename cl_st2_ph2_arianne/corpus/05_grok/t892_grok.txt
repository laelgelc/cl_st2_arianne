Today is World Meat Free Day, a perfect opportunity to celebrate the positive impact we can all make by reducing our meat consumption. By cutting back on meat, we can benefit our personal health and help protect the planet.

There are many ways to get started, such as joining Meat Free Mondays, adopting a flexitarian lifestyle, or opting for higher-quality meat when we do eat it. The key is that even small changes in our habits can add up to a huge global impact.
