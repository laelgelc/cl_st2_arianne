The protesters have highlighted the government's evictions, abuses, and lack of consultation with their community. They are vowing to continue fighting for self-determination in their ancestral lands.

Greenpeace Thailand stands in support of these indigenous leaders, underscoring that recognizing indigenous rights is essential for effective environmental conservation.
