Greenpeace is setting off on an exciting expedition aboard our ship, the Esperanza, heading to the Sargasso Sea. This unique ecosystem lies in the Bermuda Triangle, known for its clear waters and endemic life. But today, it's under serious threat from plastic pollution that's getting trapped in ocean gyres.

These plastics are causing real harm to wildlife, including turtles, whales, and fish, and they're even making their way into the food chain. To help this vital area recover, we're calling for the creation of ocean sanctuaries through a new Global Ocean Treaty.
