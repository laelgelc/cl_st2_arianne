In China, former city dweller Hou Xueying has embraced organic farming. She uses ducks to fertilize her rice fields and sells her produce online. She also plans to educate children about where food comes from.

Li Yang points out that consumers can play a role in food safety by opting for ecological produce.

In Thailand, fisherman Jirasak Meerit changed his approach after noticing declining fish stocks from destructive methods. He guided his community to implement sustainable regulations, such as catching only mature sea animals, which has led to increased catches.

The films "A Sustainable Catch" and "Farmed With Love" are finalists in the Real Food Films contest.
