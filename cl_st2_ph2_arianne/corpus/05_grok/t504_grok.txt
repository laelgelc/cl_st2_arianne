2019 stands out as an iconic year in the fight against climate change. Millions of people mobilized worldwide, taking to the streets and raising their voices to demand action. They called out corporations and governments for their insufficient efforts to protect the planet.

Yet, it was also a devastating time. Extreme weather events, fueled by climate change, ravaged countries around the globe, bringing destruction and hardship to countless communities.

Despite these challenges, 2019 marked a landmark of people power. Communities united, standing strong against adversities to push for a greener, more sustainable, and hopeful future.
