In 2020, the MV Wakashio oil spill in Mauritius resulted in 1,000 tonnes of fuel leaking into a lagoon near a marine reserve. This incident harmed local communities and marine life.

Disasters like this highlight the urgent need to ban dangerous fuels in fragile areas, such as the Arctic, and to transition shipping away from fossil fuels.
