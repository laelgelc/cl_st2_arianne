Our recent Antarctic expedition set out to observe incredible wildlife, including penguins and whales, in one of the planet's most remote regions. What we discovered, however, was deeply concerning: microplastics in water samples and hazardous chemicals in snow, even in freshly fallen snow. These findings reveal just how widespread pollution has become, reaching even this isolated part of the world.
