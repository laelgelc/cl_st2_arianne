Authors, journalists, poets, and playwrights improve the planet by freely expressing ideas, as free speech is essential for positive change. Over 100 authors have pledged to defend free speech and those peacefully protecting forests, in response to multimillion-dollar lawsuits by Canadian company Resolute Forest Products aimed at silencing Greenpeace and Stand.earth's exposure of its controversial boreal forest logging. The post shares quotes from some authors, links to more signatories, and thanks supporters for celebrating freedom of expression and advocacy.
