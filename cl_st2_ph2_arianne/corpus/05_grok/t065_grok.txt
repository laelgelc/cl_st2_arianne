In a world facing unprecedented climate challenges, social media has become a powerful tool for amplifying voices and driving change. Here, we spotlight nine inspiring climate activists from around the globe who are using platforms like Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok to raise awareness about climate issues and motivate collective action.

Edwin Namakanga, from Uganda, draws inspiration from the direct impacts of climate change in his community and from fellow activists like Vanessa Nakate. His work focuses on highlighting these realities and encouraging global solidarity.

Fabian Grischkat, based in Germany, advocates passionately for both climate action and LGBTQ+ rights, weaving these interconnected causes into his online messaging to build inclusive movements.

Lillian Ono, from Japan, connects climate change to broader systemic injustices, using her platform to expose how environmental degradation exacerbates inequalities and calling for holistic solutions.

Rose Kobusinge, also from Uganda, emphasizes amplifying African voices in the climate conversation while promoting sustainable energy initiatives that empower local communities.

Tori Tsui, with roots in Hong Kong and the UK, explores the intersections between mental health and the climate crisis, advocating for emotional well-being as a key part of environmental activism.

Venetia La Manna, from the UK, takes on the fast fashion industry, challenging its environmental toll and urging consumers toward more ethical and sustainable choices.

Amanda Costa, from Brazil, links climate justice with racial justice, drawing attention to how marginalized groups bear the brunt of environmental harm and pushing for equitable responses.

Ayisha Siddiqa, from Pakistan, fights against major polluters while championing the rights of marginalized communities, using her influence to demand accountability and support for those most affected.

Camille Etienne, from France, lobbies against harmful projects like pipelines and mining operations, mobilizing support to protect ecosystems and prevent further exploitation.

These activists represent the diverse, global face of climate advocacy, each bringing unique perspectives to inspire action through their social media presence.
