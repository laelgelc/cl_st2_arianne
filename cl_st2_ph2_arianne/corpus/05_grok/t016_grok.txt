Germany has achieved a major milestone with the shutdown of its last nuclear reactor on April 15, 2023, following decades of persistent protests. This closure paves the way for an accelerated transition to renewable energy sources.

A landmark Global Ocean Treaty has been secured, enabling the creation of marine sanctuaries and aiming to protect 30% of the world's oceans by 2030.

The United Nations General Assembly has requested an advisory opinion from the International Court of Justice on states' climate duties, an initiative driven by students from Pacific Islands nations.

Starting in 2025, Schiphol Airport in the Netherlands will ban private jets and night flights to address pollution concerns.
