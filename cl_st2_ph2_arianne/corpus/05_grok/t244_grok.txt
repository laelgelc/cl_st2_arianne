This situation underscores the importance of solidarity between environmental and labor movements. Examples include support for striking workers and unions that are demanding decent jobs.

At COP26, Greenpeace is calling for ambitious commitments to advance these goals. In the US, Greenpeace supports Biden's jobs plan, the PRO Act, and the creation of unionized green jobs as steps toward building a just society.
