## 4. Human vs LLM usage

Humans have the highest *positive* scores on this factor, meaning they are **least associated** with this negative-pole lexicon. Gemini persona texts are most associated with it.

This suggests that, under a “Greenpeace writer” persona prompt, Gemini tends to:

- Over-index on **Greenpeace’s iconic ships, founders, and dramatic actions**, and
- Use a **heroic, movement-mythology register** (Rainbow Warrior, Arctic Sunrise, bombing, Arctic 30, courage, determination).

So this pole captures a **Gemini-specific stylisation of Greenpeace as a heroic, ship-centred, struggle narrative**, more than it captures the everyday register of human-authored Greenpeace blogs.

## 5. Interpreting the discourse dimension

Bringing loadings and examples together, the negative pole encodes a discourse that is:

- **Movement- and organisation-centric** (Greenpeace’s identity, history, fleet, founders, martyrs).
- **Action- and conflict-oriented** (protests, arrests, legal defence, opposition, marches, state repression).
- **Heroic and commemorative in tone** (courage, determination, spirit, persistence; anniversaries, memorials).
- **Symbolically rich** (Rainbow Warrior, Arctic Sunrise, Cree myth, “warriors of the rainbow,” vessels of peace).

It is less about abstract climate policy or technical detail, and more about **telling the story of Greenpeace as a courageous, united movement engaged in high-stakes struggle**.

## 7. Recommended primary label

If one concise label is needed, the most encompassing and corpus-grounded is:

**Greenpeace Movement Mythos and Heroic Activism**

- “Greenpeace Movement” captures the organisational, ship- and founder-centred focus.
- “Mythos” captures the commemorative, symbolic, and iconic elements (Rainbow Warrior, Arctic Sunrise, Cree myth, anniversaries).
- “Heroic Activism” captures the lexicon of courage, determination, opposition, police, defense, marches, and the narrative of struggle and resilience.

This label reflects both the **lexical profile** (ships, founders, courage, opposition, police, defense) and the **discursive function** seen in the examples: constructing Greenpeace as a united, courageous movement engaged in emblematic struggles that define its identity.
